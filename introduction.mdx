Mi Tienda Payment Checkout is a production-ready fullstack application that lets customers select a product, enter delivery and card payment details, and receive a real-time payment result. The backend processes transactions through the [Wompi](https://sandbox.wompi.co) payment gateway while the frontend handles card tokenization directly — card data never reaches your servers.

## What the app does

The checkout flow progresses through seven distinct stages:

1. The customer selects a product from the catalog.
2. They fill in card and delivery details.
3. They review a payment summary.
4. The browser tokenizes the card directly with Wompi.
5. The frontend sends only the token to the backend for server-side processing.
6. The backend polls until the transaction resolves.
7. The customer sees an `APPROVED`, `DECLINED`, or `ERROR` result.

<Note>
  Card data is never sent to the backend. The browser calls Wompi's tokenization endpoint directly using your public key, and only the resulting `cardToken` is forwarded to your API.
</Note>

## Wompi security model

Wompi's security model separates public-key operations from private-key operations:

- **Client-side (browser):** Uses `VITE_PAYMENT_PUBLIC_KEY` to call `POST /tokens/cards` directly on Wompi. The response is a short-lived `cardToken`.
- **Server-side (NestJS):** Uses `PAYMENT_PRIVATE_KEY` to call Wompi's transaction endpoint. An integrity hash is computed as `SHA256(reference + amount + "COP" + integrityKey)` to prevent tampering.
- **Acceptance token:** Retrieved by the backend via `GET /merchants/{publicKey}` and returned to the frontend before payment submission.

This split means your private key is never exposed to the browser, and raw card numbers never touch your database.
